Elote - Street Corn Salad

Description

A simple and refreshing take on Elote, or Mexican Street Corn, perfect for pairing with summer meals on the grill!

Ingredients

  • 1000g or kernels from 8 ears sweet corn, cut from cobs
  • Neutral oil (avocado, canola, grapeseed, etc)
  • Salt
  • 20g/1 jalapeno, small dice
  • 35-40g or 1 bunch of scallions (middle green part only)
  • 1 lime zest and juice
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced/pressed
  • 100g or 1/2c mayo
  • 50g or 1/4c sour cream
  • 2g or 1tsp black pepper
  • 2g or 1tsp chili powder

Steps

  1. Preheat large cast iron over high, add about 3Tbsp neutral oil
  2. Add corn kernels and a large pinch of salt
  3. Cook for 90 seconds, then toss. Cook for 3-5 more minutes, tossing every 90 seconds
  4. When corn begins to pop and has started to caramelize, it should be done
  5. Transfer to bowl and allow to cool slightly
  6. add in jalapeno, scallions, cilantro, lime zest and juice, garlic, mayo, sour cream, pepper, chili powder, and cheese
  7. Stir to combine
  8. Taste for seasoning, add salt or lime juice to taste if needed
  9. Garnish with additional cheese and serve
