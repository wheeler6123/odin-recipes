Baked Beans

Description

Smoky tender baked beans go great with smoked or grilled meats!

Ingredients

  • 175g/6oz chopped bacon
  • 150g (1 medium) white onion, small diced
  • 50g (2 small) jalapenos, small diced
  • 15g (5-6 cloves) garlic, minced
  • 325g or 1.5c ketchup
  • 100g or 1/4c molasses
  • 50g or 1/4c brown sugar
  • 30g or 1 3/4 Tbsp worcestershire
  • 30g or 2Tbsp yellow mustard
  • 30g or 2Tbsp hot sauce
  • 20g 1Tbsp + 1/2tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 7g or 1Tbsp chili powder
  • 2g or 1tsp black pepper
  • 2x 425g//15oz cans cannellini beans with liquid
  • 4x 425g/15oz cans cannellini beans, rinsed
  • 200-400g or 3/4 - 1.5c water

Steps

  1. Add bacon to a heavy bottomed pot over medium heat
  2. Stir and render fat for 10-15 minutes until golden
  3. Add in diced onion, jalapeno, and garlic
  4. Stir, scraping up any bacon fond
  5. Cook for about 10 min or until veggies have softened
  6. Add ketchup, molasses, brown sugar, worcestershire, mustard, hot sauce, vinegar, chili powder, and pepper. Stir to combine
  7. Add beans and about 200g water, stir
  8. Add additional 200g of water if needed
  9. Gently stir and bring to a simmer
  10. Load into a 350F oven to bake for 90 minutes, gently stirring 2-3 times during baking
  11. Beans are done when thickened, but still a little saucy
  12. Add water or sauce to thin slightly if needed
